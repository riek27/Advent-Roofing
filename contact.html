Frequently Asked Questions

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the roof, but most residential roof installations take 2-5 days. Factors like weather conditions, roof pitch, and material type can affect the timeline. We provide a detailed schedule during our initial consultation.

We offer a wide range of roofing materials including asphalt shingles (3-tab and architectural), metal roofing (standing seam and corrugated), clay and concrete tiles, slate, synthetic roofing, and flat roofing systems. We'll help you choose the best option for your budget, style, and climate.

Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make your roofing project more affordable. We work with several financing partners to provide competitive rates and payment plans. Contact us for more information about our financing options.

We provide two types of warranties: manufacturer warranties on materials (ranging from 20-50 years depending on the product) and our workmanship warranty (typically 10 years). All warranties are provided in writing before work begins.

During our free inspection, we'll assess your roof's condition and provide honest recommendations. Generally, if damage is isolated to a small area, repairs may suffice. If the roof is near the end of its lifespan or has widespread damage, replacement is usually more cost-effective long-term.
