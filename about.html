About Advent Roofing

Two decades of excellence in roofing solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2003, Advent Roofing began as a small family-owned business with a simple mission: to provide exceptional roofing services with integrity, quality, and reliability. What started as a two-person operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted roofing companies.

Over the past 20 years, we've completed thousands of roofing projects, from simple repairs to complex commercial installations. Our commitment to using premium materials, employing skilled craftsmen, and delivering outstanding customer service has earned us a reputation for excellence.

Today, we continue to uphold the values that our founders established: honesty, quality workmanship, and putting our customers first in everything we do.
